How it works

From your door to clean in four steps

We handle the pickup, the laundromat, and the delivery. You handle nothing.

1

Schedule a pickup

Open the app, set your address and preferences — detergent, temperature, fold style — and pick a time. Takes 60 seconds.

2

We collect your laundry

A TidyFolds driver comes to your door with a labeled bag. Leave your laundry outside — they handle the rest.

3

A local laundromat washes it

Your clothes go to a vetted partner nearby. Washed, dried, and folded exactly to your preferences.

4

Clean clothes at your door

Your driver returns the next morning — or same day if you choose express. Fresh, folded, ready.

FAQ

Questions people actually ask

How long does it take? ▾
Standard orders are returned the next morning — most within 18–22 hours. Express orders are returned within 4 hours. We confirm your expected window when you place the order.
What if something gets lost or damaged? ▾
TidyFolds covers damage claims up to $300 per order. Contact support within 48 hours of delivery and we'll make it right — no runaround, no fine print.
Can I request a specific detergent? ▾
Yes. Your preference card lets you choose from common detergents or request fragrance-free washing. You can also flag allergens. Preferences are saved and applied to every order automatically.
How does TidyFolds+ pay for itself? ▾
Each delivery costs $4.99. TidyFolds+ is $14.99/month and removes the delivery fee on every order. At 3 pickups per month you break even. Most Plus members do 4–6 pickups monthly, saving $5–$15 over pay-per-use.
Which areas do you serve? ▾
We currently serve Edmond and Oklahoma City. Expanding to Tulsa and Norman in early 2026. Enter your address in the app to check coverage in your specific neighborhood.
Can I cancel TidyFolds+ anytime? ▾
Yes — cancel anytime from the app, no phone call required. Cancel before your 30-day trial ends and you'll never be charged. No cancellation fees, ever.

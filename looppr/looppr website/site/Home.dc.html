How it works

From your door to clean, in four steps

We handle the pickup, the laundromat, and the delivery. You handle nothing.

1

Schedule a pickup

Set your address, detergent, temperature and fold style, then pick a window. Takes 60 seconds.

2

A Looppr driver comes to your door with a labeled bag. Leave it out — they take it from there.

3

A local laundromat washes it

Your clothes go to a vetted partner nearby — washed, dried and folded to your saved preferences.

4

Clean clothes at your door

Your driver returns the next morning — or same day with express. Fresh, folded, ready.
